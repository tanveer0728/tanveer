Types of Online Games

Text-Based Games

Early online games such as MUDs (Multi-User Dungeons). Evolution from simple text interfaces to more complex narratives. Impact on the development of later online RPGs (Role-Playing Games).

Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Games (MMORPGs)

Milestones like "Ultima Online" and "EverQuest". The phenomenon of "World of Warcraft" and its influence on the genre. Current popular MMORPGs and their community dynamics.

First-Person Shooters (FPS)

Historical development from "Doom" and "Quake" to modern FPS games. Key features and innovations introduced by games like "Counter-Strike" and "Call of Duty." Current trends and popular titles in the FPS genre.

Multiplayer Online Battle Arenas (MOBAs)

Origins and rise of MOBAs with games like "Defense of the Ancients" (DotA). The success of "League of Legends" and "Dota 2." Analysis of the competitive scene and eSports influence.

Battle Royale Games

The emergence of the battle royale genre with games like "PUBG" (PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds). The explosive popularity of "Fortnite" and its cultural impact. Current leading battle royale games and player engagement.

Casual and Social Games

Development of browser-based games and social media games. The success of games like "FarmVille" and "Candy Crush Saga." Current trends in mobile gaming and casual online games.

Racing Games

From arcade classics like "Need for Speed" to realistic simulators like "Gran Turismo". The evolution of racing games and their impact on gaming culture.

Strategy Games

From turn-based classics like "Civilization" to real-time strategy games like "StarCraft". Explore the history and evolution of strategy games and their diverse gameplay mechanics.

Puzzle Games

From traditional puzzles like "Tetris" to innovative mobile games like "2048". Discover the variety and creativity within the puzzle game genre and their enduring popularity.

Sports Games

From classic sports simulations like "FIFA" and "NBA 2K" to arcade-style titles like "Mario Kart". Explore the diverse range of sports games
